# Product Management Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (34)

### Product Management Fundamentals
1. **Inspired** — Marty Cagan (2nd ed, 2018), Wiley. How to create products customers love.
2. **Empowered** — Marty Cagan & Chris Jones (2020), Wiley. Building empowered product teams.
3. **Transformed** — Marty Cagan (2024), Wiley. Moving to the product operating model.
4. **The Product Book** — Product School (2017). Comprehensive PM guide.
5. **Cracking the PM Interview** — Gayle McDowell & Jackie Bavaro (2013). PM career guide.
6. **Product Management in Practice** — Matt LeMay (2nd ed, 2022), O'Reilly. Day-to-day PM work.
7. **Build** — Tony Fadell (2022), Harper Business. Lessons from iPod/iPhone creator.

### Product Strategy
8. **Good Strategy Bad Strategy** — Richard Rumelt (2011), Crown. Strategy fundamentals.
9. **Playing to Win** — A.G. Lafley & Roger Martin (2013), HBR Press. Strategic choices.
10. **7 Powers** — Hamilton Helmer (2016). Business strategy and moats.
11. **Crossing the Chasm** — Geoffrey Moore (3rd ed, 2014), Harper Business. Technology adoption.
12. **Blue Ocean Strategy** — W. Chan Kim & Renée Mauborgne (2015), HBR Press. Market creation.
13. **Obviously Awesome** — April Dunford (2019). Product positioning.
14. **The Mom Test** — Rob Fitzpatrick (2013). Customer conversation skills.

### Product Discovery
15. **Continuous Discovery Habits** — Teresa Torres (2021), Product Talk. Ongoing discovery.
16. **Testing Business Ideas** — David Bland & Alex Osterwalder (2019), Wiley. Experiment design.
17. **Sprint** — Jake Knapp (2016), Simon & Schuster. Design sprint methodology.
18. **Lean Startup** — Eric Ries (2011), Crown. Build-measure-learn.
19. **Running Lean** — Ash Maurya (3rd ed, 2022), O'Reilly. Lean canvas and validation.
20. **Competing Against Luck** — Clayton Christensen et al. (2016), Harper Business. JTBD theory.

### Growth
21. **Hacking Growth** — Sean Ellis & Morgan Brown (2017), Crown. Growth methodology.
22. **Product-Led Growth** — Wes Bush (2019). PLG strategy and tactics.
23. **Hooked** — Nir Eyal (2014), Portfolio. Habit-forming products.
24. **Lean Analytics** — Alistair Croll & Benjamin Yoskovitz (2013), O'Reilly. Metrics for startups.
25. **The Cold Start Problem** — Andrew Chen (2021), Harper Business. Network effects.

### Execution & Leadership
26. **Shape Up** — Ryan Singer (2019), Basecamp. Alternative to scrum.
27. **Outcomes Over Output** — Josh Seiden (2019). Outcome-focused product work.
28. **Measure What Matters** — John Doerr (2018), Portfolio. OKRs.
29. **Team Topologies** — Skelton & Pais (2019), IT Revolution. Team organization.
30. **Radical Candor** — Kim Scott (2017), St. Martin's. Feedback and leadership.

### Platform & Technical PM
31. **Platform Revolution** — Parker, Van Alstyne, Choudary (2016), Norton. Platform business models.
32. **API Design Patterns** — JJ Geewax (2021), Manning. API product design.
33. **Working Backwards** — Colin Bryar & Bill Carr (2021), St. Martin's. Amazon product process.

### Pricing & Monetization
34. **Monetizing Innovation** — Madhavan Ramanujam & Georg Tacke (2016), Wiley. Pricing strategy.
