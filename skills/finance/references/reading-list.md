# Finance Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (32)

### Financial Modeling & Analysis
1. **Financial Modeling and Valuation** — Paul Pignataro (2013), Wiley. Investment banking modeling.
2. **Financial Intelligence** — Karen Berman & Joe Knight (2013), HBR Press. Finance for non-finance.
3. **The Interpretation of Financial Statements** — Benjamin Graham (1998), Harper Business.
4. **Valuation** — McKinsey & Company (7th ed, 2020), Wiley. Corporate valuation bible.
5. **Investment Banking** — Joshua Rosenbaum & Joshua Pearl (3rd ed, 2020), Wiley.
6. **Financial Statements** — Thomas Ittelson (3rd ed, 2020). Visual guide to statements.

### SaaS Finance & Metrics
7. **Scaling Lean** — Ash Maurya (2016), Portfolio. Metrics-driven growth.
8. **Lean Analytics** — Alistair Croll & Benjamin Yoskovitz (2013), O'Reilly. Startup metrics.
9. **The SaaS Playbook** — Rob Walling (2023). SaaS business fundamentals.
10. **SaaS Metrics 2.0** — David Skok (online, updated 2024). Comprehensive SaaS metrics guide.
11. **Subscribed** — Tien Tzuo (2018), Portfolio. Subscription economy.
12. **The Automatic Customer** — John Warrillow (2015), Portfolio. Recurring revenue models.

### Corporate Finance
13. **Principles of Corporate Finance** — Brealey, Myers & Allen (14th ed, 2022), McGraw-Hill.
14. **Corporate Finance** — Jonathan Berk & Peter DeMarzo (6th ed, 2023), Pearson.
15. **The CFO Guidebook** — Steven Bragg (4th ed, 2022), AccountingTools.
16. **Financial Planning & Analysis and Performance Management** — Jack Alexander (2018), Wiley.

### Fundraising & Venture Capital
17. **Venture Deals** — Brad Feld & Jason Mendelson (4th ed, 2019), Wiley. VC term sheets.
18. **Secrets of Sand Hill Road** — Scott Kupor (2019), Portfolio. VC industry guide.
19. **The Business of Venture Capital** — Mahendra Ramsinghani (3rd ed, 2021), Wiley.
20. **Mastering the VC Game** — Jeffrey Bussgang (2010), Portfolio.
21. **Angel** — Jason Calacanis (2017), Harper Business. Angel investing.
22. **Super Founders** — Ali Tamaseb (2021), PublicAffairs. Data on successful founders.

### Accounting
23. **Accounting Made Simple** — Mike Piper (2013). Accounting basics.
24. **Revenue Recognition: Principles and Practices** — Steven Bragg (2021), AccountingTools.
25. **The Controller's Function** — Steven Bragg (5th ed, 2020), Wiley.

### Treasury & Cash Management
26. **Treasury Management** — Steven Bragg (2010), Wiley. Corporate treasury.
27. **Working Capital Management** — Lorenzo Preve & Virginia Sarria-Allende (2010), Oxford.

### Strategy & Decision Making
28. **The Outsiders** — William Thorndike (2012), HBR Press. Capital allocation.
29. **Good to Great** — Jim Collins (2001), Harper Business. Company performance.
30. **Thinking in Bets** — Annie Duke (2018), Portfolio. Decision-making under uncertainty.
31. **The Most Important Thing** — Howard Marks (2011), Columbia. Investment thinking.
32. **Measure What Matters** — John Doerr (2018), Portfolio. OKRs and goal setting.
