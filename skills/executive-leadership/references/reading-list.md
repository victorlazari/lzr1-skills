# Executive Leadership Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (32)

### CEO & Strategy
1. **The Hard Thing About Hard Things** — Ben Horowitz (2014), Harper Business. CEO challenges.
2. **High Output Management** — Andy Grove (1983), Vintage. Management fundamentals.
3. **Good Strategy Bad Strategy** — Richard Rumelt (2011), Crown. Strategy.
4. **The Great CEO Within** — Matt Mochary (2019). CEO operations.
5. **Only the Paranoid Survive** — Andy Grove (1996), Currency. Strategic inflection points.
6. **Zero to One** — Peter Thiel (2014), Crown. Building the future.
7. **The Innovator's Dilemma** — Clayton Christensen (1997), HBR Press. Disruption.
8. **Playing to Win** — A.G. Lafley & Roger Martin (2013), HBR Press. Strategy.

### Technology Leadership
9. **An Elegant Puzzle** — Will Larson (2019), Stripe Press. Engineering management.
10. **Staff Engineer** — Will Larson (2021), Stripe Press. Technical leadership.
11. **The Manager's Path** — Camille Fournier (2017), O'Reilly. Engineering leadership.
12. **Accelerate** — Forsgren, Humble & Kim (2018), IT Revolution. DevOps metrics.
13. **Team Topologies** — Skelton & Pais (2019), IT Revolution. Org design.
14. **The Phoenix Project** — Gene Kim et al. (2013), IT Revolution. IT leadership.
15. **Empowered** — Marty Cagan (2020), Wiley. Product leadership.

### Financial Leadership
16. **Venture Deals** — Brad Feld & Jason Mendelson (4th ed, 2019), Wiley.
17. **Measure What Matters** — John Doerr (2018), Portfolio. OKRs.
18. **The SaaS CFO** — Ben Murray (2023, online). SaaS finance.
19. **Financial Intelligence** — Berman & Knight (2013), HBR Press.
20. **Scaling Up** — Verne Harnish (2014), Gazelles. Growth finance.

### Organizational Leadership
21. **Radical Candor** — Kim Scott (2017), St. Martin's Press. Feedback.
22. **The Five Dysfunctions of a Team** — Patrick Lencioni (2002), Jossey-Bass.
23. **Turn the Ship Around!** — L. David Marquet (2013), Portfolio.
24. **Dare to Lead** — Brene Brown (2018), Random House. Vulnerability in leadership.
25. **No Rules Rules** — Reed Hastings (2020), Penguin. Netflix culture.
26. **Powerful** — Patty McCord (2018), Missionday. Culture building.

### Scaling & Growth
27. **Blitzscaling** — Reid Hoffman & Chris Yeh (2018), Currency.
28. **The Lean Startup** — Eric Ries (2011), Crown.
29. **Crossing the Chasm** — Geoffrey Moore (3rd ed, 2014), Harper Business.
30. **From Impossible to Inevitable** — Aaron Ross & Jason Lemkin (2nd ed, 2019), Wiley.

### Decision-Making
31. **Thinking, Fast and Slow** — Daniel Kahneman (2011), Farrar Straus Giroux.
32. **The Art of Action** — Stephen Bungay (2011), Nicholas Brealey. Strategy execution.
