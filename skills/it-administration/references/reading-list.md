# IT Administration Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (32)

### IT Operations & Management
1. **The Practice of System and Network Administration** — Limoncelli, Hogan & Chalup (3rd ed, 2016), Addison-Wesley.
2. **The Practice of Cloud System Administration** — Limoncelli, Chalup & Hogan (2014), Addison-Wesley.
3. **The Visible Ops Handbook** — Kim, Behr & Spafford (2004), IT Process Institute.
4. **IT Service Management (ITIL 4)** — Axelos (2019). ITSM framework.
5. **Site Reliability Engineering** — Beyer, Jones, Petoff & Murphy (2016), O'Reilly.
6. **The Phoenix Project** — Gene Kim et al. (2013), IT Revolution.

### Identity & Access Management
7. **Identity Attack Vectors** — Haber & Hibbert (2020), Apress. IAM security.
8. **Solving Identity Management in Modern Applications** — Hardt (2nd ed, 2022), Apress.
9. **OAuth 2 in Action** — Richer & Sanso (2017), Manning.
10. **Identity and Data Security for Web Development** — LeBlanc & Messerschmidt (2016), O'Reilly.
11. **Zero Trust Networks** — Gilman & Barth (2nd ed, 2024), O'Reilly.

### Endpoint Security
12. **Endpoint Security** — Anson (2020), Syngress. Endpoint protection.
13. **macOS Security and Privacy Guide** — drduh (ongoing), GitHub. macOS hardening.
14. **Windows Security Monitoring** — Miroshnikov (2018), Wiley.
15. **The Art of Mac Malware** — Patrick Wardle (2022). macOS threats.

### IT Security
16. **Security Engineering** — Ross Anderson (3rd ed, 2020), Wiley.
17. **Defensive Security Handbook** — Brotherston & Berlin (2nd ed, 2024), O'Reilly.
18. **Blue Team Handbook** — Don Murdoch (2019). Incident response.
19. **Cybersecurity Ops with bash** — Troncone & Albing (2019), O'Reilly.
20. **Practical Cybersecurity Architecture** — Magnusson (2022), Packt.

### Cloud & SaaS Administration
21. **Google Workspace Admin Guide** — Google (ongoing). Official admin docs.
22. **Microsoft 365 Administration Inside Out** — Orin Thomas (2023), Microsoft Press.
23. **Okta Administration: Up and Running** — various (2023). Okta admin.
24. **Cloud Security and Privacy** — Mather, Kumaraswamy & Latif (2009), O'Reilly.

### Compliance & Governance
25. **IT Governance** — Peter Weill & Jeanne Ross (2004), HBS Press.
26. **SOC 2 Compliance Handbook** — various (2023). SOC 2 guide.
27. **NIST Cybersecurity Framework** — NIST (2024). CSF 2.0.
28. **CIS Controls** — Center for Internet Security (v8, 2021). Security controls.

### Automation & Scripting
29. **Automate the Boring Stuff with Python** — Al Sweigart (2nd ed, 2019), No Starch.
30. **PowerShell for Sysadmins** — Adam Bertram (2020), No Starch.
31. **Learn Bash the Hard Way** — Ian Miell (2019). Shell scripting.
32. **Ansible for DevOps** — Jeff Geerling (2022). IT automation.
