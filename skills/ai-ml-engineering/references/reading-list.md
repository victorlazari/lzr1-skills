# AI/ML Recommended Reading List (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (32)

### LLM & AI Engineering
1. **AI Engineering: Building Applications with Foundation Models** — Chip Huyen (2025), O'Reilly. End-to-end foundation model application development.
2. **Hands-On Large Language Models** — Jay Alammar & Maarten Grootendorst (2024), O'Reilly. Practical LLM building with 300 original figures.
3. **LLM Engineer's Handbook** — Paul Iusztin & Maxime Labonne (2024), Packt. LLM design, training, and deployment with MLOps.
4. **Building LLM Powered Applications** — Valentina Alto (2024), Packt. LangChain, prompt engineering, practical LLM apps.
5. **Build a Large Language Model (From Scratch)** — Sebastian Raschka (2024), Manning. Ground-up LLM construction and training.
6. **Prompt Engineering for Generative AI** — James Phoenix & Mike Taylor (2024), O'Reilly. Systematic prompt design methodology.

### Machine Learning Systems
7. **Designing Machine Learning Systems** — Chip Huyen (2022), O'Reilly. Holistic ML system design for production.
8. **Machine Learning Engineering in Action** — Ben Wilson (2022), Manning. Production ML engineering practices.
9. **Reliable Machine Learning** — Cathy Chen et al. (2022), O'Reilly. ML reliability patterns.
10. **Machine Learning Design Patterns** — Valliappa Lakshmanan et al. (2020), O'Reilly. 30 proven ML design patterns.
11. **Fundamentals of Data Engineering** — Joe Reis & Matt Housley (2022), O'Reilly. Data infrastructure for ML.

### MLOps & Production
12. **Practical MLOps** — Noah Gift & Alfredo Deza (2021), O'Reilly. ML operations automation.
13. **Introducing MLOps** — Mark Treveil et al. (2020), O'Reilly. MLOps fundamentals and practices.
14. **MLOps Engineering at Scale** — Carl Osipov (2022), Manning. Enterprise MLOps patterns.
15. **Effective Data Science Infrastructure** — Ville Tuulos (2022), Manning. ML infrastructure with Metaflow.
16. **Kubeflow for Machine Learning** — Trevor Grant et al. (2020), O'Reilly. ML pipelines on Kubernetes.

### Deep Learning & Neural Networks
17. **Deep Learning** — Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, Aaron Courville, MIT Press. Foundational deep learning reference.
18. **Generative Deep Learning** — David Foster (2nd ed, 2023), O'Reilly. GANs, VAEs, Transformers, Diffusion Models.
19. **Production-Ready Applied Deep Learning** — Tomasz Palczewski et al. (2024), Packt. DL in production environments.
20. **Mathematics for Machine Learning** — Deisenroth, Faisal, Ong (2020), Cambridge University Press. Mathematical foundations.

### NLP & Language
21. **Natural Language Processing with Transformers** — Lewis Tunstall et al. (Revised 2024), O'Reilly. Hugging Face ecosystem.
22. **Transformers for Natural Language Processing** — Denis Rothman (2nd ed, 2024), Packt. Transformer architectures deep dive.
23. **Speech and Language Processing** — Jurafsky & Martin (3rd ed draft, 2024). Comprehensive NLP textbook.

### Computer Vision
24. **Foundations of Computer Vision** — Antonio Torralba et al. (2024), MIT Press. Latest CV textbook.
25. **Computer Vision: Algorithms and Applications** — Richard Szeliski (2nd ed, 2022), Springer. Comprehensive CV reference.

### Reinforcement Learning
26. **Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction** — Sutton & Barto (2nd ed), MIT Press. Foundational RL text.
27. **Grokking Deep Reinforcement Learning** — Miguel Morales (2020), Manning. Practical RL implementation.

### Probabilistic ML & Theory
28. **Probabilistic Machine Learning: Advanced Topics** — Kevin Murphy (2023), MIT Press. Advanced ML theory.
29. **Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning** — Christopher Bishop, Springer. Classical ML theory.

### AI Strategy & Ethics
30. **Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach** — Russell & Norvig (4th ed), Pearson. Authoritative AI textbook.
31. **The Alignment Problem** — Brian Christian (2020), W.W. Norton. AI safety and alignment.
32. **Atlas of AI** — Kate Crawford (2021), Yale University Press. Social and political implications of AI.
