# Supply Chain & Procurement Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (32)

### Supply Chain Management
1. **Supply Chain Management** — Chopra & Meindl (7th ed, 2019), Pearson.
2. **The New Supply Chain Agenda** — Slone, Dittmann & Mentzer (2010), HBS Press.
3. **Supply Chain Revolution** — Suman Sarkar (2017), AMACOM.
4. **The Resilient Enterprise** — Yossi Sheffi (2005), MIT Press.
5. **The Power of Resilience** — Yossi Sheffi (2015), MIT Press.
6. **End-to-End Supply Chain** — Alexandre Oliveira (2022), Kogan Page.

### Procurement & Sourcing
7. **Procurement and Supply Chain Management** — Lysons & Farrington (10th ed, 2020), Pearson.
8. **Strategic Sourcing Management** — Handfield et al. (2011), Auerbach.
9. **The Procurement and Supply Manager's Desk Reference** — Sollish & Semanik (3rd ed, 2020), Wiley.
10. **Category Management in Purchasing** — O'Brien (4th ed, 2019), Kogan Page.
11. **Negotiation Genius** — Malhotra & Bazerman (2007), Bantam. Negotiation.
12. **Getting to Yes** — Fisher, Ury & Patton (3rd ed, 2011), Penguin. Negotiation.

### Analytics & Optimization
13. **Supply Chain Analytics** — Srinivasan (2022), Springer.
14. **Demand-Driven Supply Chain Management** — Simon Eagle (2nd ed, 2022), Kogan Page.
15. **Inventory Management Explained** — David Piasecki (2009), Ops Publishing.
16. **The Goal** — Eliyahu Goldratt (1984), North River Press. Theory of constraints.
17. **Factory Physics** — Hopp & Spearman (3rd ed, 2011), Waveland. Operations science.

### Logistics & Operations
18. **Logistics & Supply Chain Management** — Martin Christopher (5th ed, 2016), Pearson.
19. **The Handbook of Logistics and Distribution Management** — Rushton et al. (6th ed, 2017), Kogan Page.
20. **Operations Management** — Heizer, Render & Munson (13th ed, 2020), Pearson.
21. **Lean Thinking** — Womack & Jones (2003), Free Press.

### Technology & Digital Supply Chain
22. **Digital Supply Chain Transformation** — Alicke et al. (2022), McKinsey/Wiley.
23. **Supply Chain 4.0** — Elouali (2023). Digital transformation.
24. **AI in Supply Chain** — various (2024). AI applications.
25. **Blockchain for Supply Chain** — Moosavi et al. (2022). Blockchain applications.

### Risk & Sustainability
26. **Supply Chain Risk Management** — Zsidisin & Henke (2019), Springer.
27. **Sustainable Supply Chains** — Bouchery et al. (2017), Springer.
28. **The Sustainable Supply Chain** — Emmett & Sood (2010), Wiley.
29. **Global Supply Chain Security** — Williams (2014), Springer.

### Vendor Management
30. **Vested** — Kate Vitasek et al. (2012), Palgrave Macmillan. Partnership model.
31. **The Outsourcing Handbook** — White & James (2nd ed, 2017), Kogan Page.
32. **Strategic Outsourcing** — Greaver (1999), AMACOM.
