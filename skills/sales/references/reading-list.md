# Sales Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (32)

### Sales Methodology
1. **SPIN Selling** — Neil Rackham (1988), McGraw-Hill. Consultative selling methodology.
2. **The Challenger Sale** — Matthew Dixon & Brent Adamson (2011), Portfolio. Teaching-based selling.
3. **Gap Selling** — Keenan (2019). Problem-centric sales methodology.
4. **MEDDICC** — Andy Whyte (2020). Enterprise qualification framework.
5. **New Sales. Simplified.** — Mike Weinberg (2012), AMACOM. Prospecting and pipeline.
6. **The Qualified Sales Leader** — John McMahon (2021). Enterprise sales leadership.
7. **Selling to VITO** — Anthony Parinello (3rd ed, 2010). Selling to executives.

### Prospecting and Pipeline
8. **Fanatical Prospecting** — Jeb Blount (2015), Wiley. Outbound methodology.
9. **Predictable Revenue** — Aaron Ross & Marylou Tyler (2011). Outbound machine.
10. **Combo Prospecting** — Tony Hughes (2018), AMACOM. Multi-channel outreach.
11. **Smart Calling** — Art Sobczak (3rd ed, 2020), Wiley. Cold calling methodology.
12. **Outbound Sales, No Fluff** — Rex Biberston & Ryan Reisert (2017). Tactical outbound.

### Negotiation and Closing
13. **Never Split the Difference** — Chris Voss (2016), Harper Business. Negotiation tactics.
14. **Getting to Yes** — Fisher, Ury & Patton (3rd ed, 2011), Penguin. Principled negotiation.
15. **Influence** — Robert Cialdini (revised ed, 2021), Harper Business. Persuasion psychology.
16. **The Transparency Sale** — Todd Caponi (2019). Honesty-based selling.

### Account Management and Customer Success
17. **The Expansion Sale** — Erik Peterson & Tim Riesterer (2020), McGraw-Hill. Growing accounts.
18. **Customer Success** — Nick Mehta et al. (2016), Wiley. CS fundamentals.
19. **Farm Don't Hunt** — Guy Nirpaz (2016). Customer success strategy.
20. **The Seven Keys to Managing Strategic Accounts** — Sherman, Sperry & Reese (2003).

### Sales Leadership
21. **Sales Management. Simplified.** — Mike Weinberg (2015), AMACOM. Managing sales teams.
22. **The Sales Acceleration Formula** — Mark Roberge (2015), Wiley. Data-driven sales scaling.
23. **Cracking the Sales Management Code** — Jason Jordan & Michelle Vazzana (2011), McGraw-Hill.
24. **Coaching Salespeople into Sales Champions** — Keith Rosen (2008), Wiley.

### Revenue Operations
25. **Revenue Operations** — Stephen Diorio & Chris Hummel (2022), Wiley. RevOps framework.
26. **Sales Engagement** — Manny Medina et al. (2019), Wiley. Modern sales tech.
27. **Blueprints for a SaaS Sales Organization** — Jacco van der Kooij & Fernando Pizarro (2018).

### Sales Engineering
28. **Mastering Technical Sales** — John Care & Aron Bohlig (3rd ed, 2014). SE methodology.
29. **The Sales Engineer Manager's Handbook** — John Care (2019). Managing SE teams.
30. **Great Demo!** — Peter Cohan (3rd ed, 2020). Demo methodology.

### Go-to-Market
31. **Product-Led Growth** — Wes Bush (2019). PLG sales motion.
32. **From Impossible to Inevitable** — Aaron Ross & Jason Lemkin (2nd ed, 2019), Wiley. Hypergrowth.
