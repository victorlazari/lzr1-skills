## 1. Account Planning

### Account Plan Template

| Section | Content |
|---|---|
| Account overview | Company, industry, size, key contacts |
| Relationship map | Stakeholders, influence, sentiment |
| Current state | Products used, adoption, health score |
| Whitespace | Unused products, departments, use cases |
| Goals | Account revenue target, expansion opportunities |
| Risks | Churn signals, competitive threats |
| Action plan | Quarterly initiatives with owners and dates |

### Account Segmentation

| Tier | Revenue | Touch Model | Review Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Strategic (Tier 1) | $500K+ ARR | High-touch, dedicated AM | Monthly EBR |
| Growth (Tier 2) | $100-500K ARR | Named AM, proactive | Quarterly EBR |
| Scale (Tier 3) | $25-100K ARR | Pooled, tech-touch + human | Bi-annual |
| Self-serve (Tier 4) | <$25K ARR | Digital-only, automated | Automated |

## 2. Customer Success

### Health Score Components

| Signal | Weight | Measurement |
|---|---|---|
| Product usage | 30% | DAU/MAU, feature adoption, login frequency |
| Support tickets | 15% | Volume, severity, resolution time |
| NPS/CSAT | 15% | Survey scores, trends |
| Relationship | 15% | Executive engagement, champion strength |
| Contract | 15% | Time to renewal, expansion history |
| Payment | 10% | On-time payment, billing issues |

### Customer Lifecycle

| Stage | Goal | Activities |
|---|---|---|
| Onboarding | Time-to-value | Implementation, training, first win |
| Adoption | Deep usage | Feature discovery, best practices |
| Expansion | Grow account | New use cases, departments, products |
| Renewal | Retain revenue | Value review, multi-year, price increase |
| Advocacy | References and referrals | Case studies, reviews, referrals |

## 3. Expansion and Upsell

### Expansion Motions

| Motion | Description | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| Seat expansion | More users on same plan | Team growth, new hires |
| Usage expansion | Higher consumption tier | Approaching limits |
| Feature upsell | Higher plan with more features | Feature requests, maturity |
| Cross-sell | Additional products | Adjacent pain points |
| Department expansion | New teams using product | Success in one team |
| Geographic expansion | New regions/offices | Company growth |

### Expansion Playbook

```
1. Identify signal (usage spike, new stakeholder, feature request)
2. Validate need (discovery conversation)
3. Quantify value (ROI of expansion)
4. Propose solution (tailored to their growth)
5. Negotiate (leverage existing relationship)
6. Close and implement (fast time-to-value)
```

## 4. Renewals

### Renewal Process Timeline

| Timing | Activity | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| 120 days out | Health check, risk assessment | CSM |
| 90 days out | Renewal conversation, value review | AM/CSM |
| 60 days out | Proposal delivered, negotiation | AM |
| 30 days out | Contract finalized, signatures | AM + Legal |
| 0 days | Renewal complete | AM |
| Post-renewal | Success plan for next period | CSM |

### Churn Prevention

| Risk Signal | Response |
|---|---|
| Declining usage | Proactive outreach, re-engagement plan |
| Support escalations | Executive involvement, fast resolution |
| Champion departure | Identify new champion, re-sell value |
| Competitive evaluation | Differentiation, executive alignment |
| Budget pressure | ROI analysis, flexible terms |
| Low NPS/CSAT | Root cause analysis, action plan |

## 5. Executive Relationships

### Executive Business Review (EBR)

```
Agenda (60 minutes):
1. Business context (10 min)
   - Their strategic priorities this quarter
   - Industry trends affecting them

2. Value delivered (15 min)
   - Metrics and ROI achieved
   - Success stories from their team

3. Adoption and usage (10 min)
   - Current state, opportunities
   - Best practices not yet adopted

4. Roadmap alignment (15 min)
   - Our upcoming features relevant to them
   - Their future needs and feedback

5. Strategic plan (10 min)
   - Joint success plan for next quarter
   - Expansion opportunities
   - Mutual commitments
```
