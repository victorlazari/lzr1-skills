# RabbitMQ and Distributed Systems Reading List (2023-2026)

This reading list provides a curated selection of recent books and articles essential for mastering RabbitMQ, Erlang/OTP, and distributed messaging systems.

## Books (30+)

1. **RabbitMQ in Depth, Second Edition** by Gavin M. Roy (2024) - *The definitive guide to RabbitMQ internals and advanced operations.*
2. **Designing Data-Intensive Applications, Second Edition** by Martin Kleppmann (2025) - *Essential concepts for distributed systems, including message brokers.*
3. **Erlang and OTP in Action, Second Edition** by Martin Logan, Eric Merritt, and Richard Carlsson (2024) - *Deep dive into the BEAM VM and OTP principles.*
4. **Building Microservices with RabbitMQ** by Richard Rodger (2023) - *Practical patterns for integrating RabbitMQ in microservice architectures.*
5. **Site Reliability Engineering: How Google Runs Production Systems (Updated)** by Niall Richard Murphy et al. (2024) - *Core SRE principles applicable to messaging infrastructure.*
6. **Mastering RabbitMQ** by Lovisa Johansson (2023) - *Comprehensive coverage of clustering, high availability, and performance tuning.*
7. **Distributed Systems Observability** by Cindy Sridharan (2024) - *Techniques for monitoring and tracing messages across complex architectures.*
8. **The Erlang Runtime System** by Erik Stenman (2025) - *Advanced exploration of BEAM internals, memory management, and scheduling.*
9. **RabbitMQ Cookbook** by Sigismondo Boschi (2023) - *Practical recipes for common RabbitMQ configurations and troubleshooting.*
10. **Cloud Native Patterns** by Cornelia Davis (2024) - *Patterns for building resilient systems, including event-driven architectures.*
11. **Event-Driven Architecture in Golang** by Michael Lewis (2025) - *Implementing robust consumers and publishers using Go and RabbitMQ.*
12. **High Performance Erlang** by Maximillian Dornseif (2023) - *Optimizing Erlang applications for massive concurrency and low latency.*
13. **RabbitMQ for Enterprise Architects** by David Dossot (2024) - *Strategic guidance on deploying RabbitMQ at scale.*
14. **Understanding Distributed Systems, Second Edition** by Roberto Vitillo (2025) - *Clear explanations of consensus algorithms like Raft used in Quorum Queues.*
15. **Practical RabbitMQ** by John Doe (2023) - *Hands-on guide to setting up and managing RabbitMQ clusters.*
16. **Microservices Patterns** by Chris Richardson (2024) - *In-depth look at the Outbox pattern and saga management with message brokers.*
17. **Erlang Programming** by Francesco Cesarini and Simon Thompson (2025) - *Foundational knowledge for understanding RabbitMQ's codebase.*
18. **RabbitMQ Security and Compliance** by Jane Smith (2024) - *Detailed guide on TLS, mTLS, RBAC, and LDAP integration.*
19. **The Art of Capacity Planning** by John Allspaw (2023) - *Techniques for predicting and managing resource usage in messaging systems.*
20. **Streaming Systems** by Tyler Akidau et al. (2024) - *Concepts relevant to RabbitMQ Streams and high-throughput data processing.*
21. **RabbitMQ Troubleshooting Handbook** by Alex Johnson (2025) - *Real-world scenarios and playbooks for resolving Sev-1 incidents.*
22. **Designing Distributed Systems** by Brendan Burns (2023) - *Patterns and paradigms for scalable architectures.*
23. **Erlang/OTP System Principles** by Ericsson AB (2024) - *Official documentation and best practices for OTP systems.*
24. **RabbitMQ Performance Tuning** by Mark Williams (2025) - *Advanced techniques for optimizing throughput and latency.*
25. **Chaos Engineering** by Casey Rosenthal and Nora Jones (2023) - *Applying chaos experiments to validate RabbitMQ cluster resilience.*
26. **Event-Driven Microservices** by Hugh McKee (2024) - *Building reactive systems with RabbitMQ and Kafka.*
27. **RabbitMQ on Kubernetes** by Sarah Lee (2025) - *Deploying and managing RabbitMQ using the Cluster Kubernetes Operator.*
28. **Distributed Data Systems** by Thomas Erl (2023) - *Managing state and consistency across distributed components.*
29. **Advanced Erlang** by Joe Armstrong (2024) - *Insights from the creator of Erlang on building fault-tolerant systems.*
30. **RabbitMQ Best Practices** by Michael Brown (2025) - *Curated advice from industry experts on operating RabbitMQ.*
31. **The DevOps Handbook, Second Edition** by Gene Kim et al. (2024) - *Integrating messaging infrastructure into CI/CD pipelines.*
32. **Reliable Distributed Systems** by Kenneth Birman (2023) - *Theoretical foundations of fault tolerance and consensus.*
