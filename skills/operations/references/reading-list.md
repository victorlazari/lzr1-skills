# Operations Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (32)

### Operations Strategy
1. **The Goal** — Eliyahu Goldratt (1984), North River Press. Theory of constraints.
2. **The Phoenix Project** — Gene Kim et al. (2013), IT Revolution. IT operations novel.
3. **The Unicorn Project** — Gene Kim (2019), IT Revolution. Developer productivity.
4. **Operations Management** — Nigel Slack et al. (9th ed, 2022), Pearson.
5. **Good Strategy Bad Strategy** — Richard Rumelt (2011), Crown. Strategy fundamentals.
6. **The Hard Thing About Hard Things** — Ben Horowitz (2014), Harper Business. Operational challenges.

### Lean and Process
7. **The Lean Startup** — Eric Ries (2011), Crown. Lean methodology for startups.
8. **Lean Thinking** — James Womack & Daniel Jones (2003), Free Press. Lean principles.
9. **The Toyota Way** — Jeffrey Liker (2nd ed, 2021), McGraw-Hill. Toyota production system.
10. **Value Stream Mapping** — Karen Martin & Mike Osterling (2014), McGraw-Hill.
11. **This Is Lean** — Niklas Modig & Par Ahlstrom (2012). Lean fundamentals.
12. **The Machine That Changed the World** — Womack, Jones & Roos (2007), Free Press.

### Project Management
13. **Inspired** — Marty Cagan (2nd ed, 2018), Wiley. Product development.
14. **Shape Up** — Ryan Singer (2019, Basecamp). Project shaping methodology.
15. **Project Management for the Unofficial Project Manager** — Kory Kogon et al. (2015), BenBella.
16. **Making Things Happen** — Scott Berkun (2008), O'Reilly. Project management.
17. **Agile Estimating and Planning** — Mike Cohn (2005), Prentice Hall.
18. **Scrum** — Jeff Sutherland (2014), Currency. Scrum methodology.

### Scaling and Growth
19. **Scaling Up** — Verne Harnish (2014), Gazelles. Growth operations.
20. **Blitzscaling** — Reid Hoffman & Chris Yeh (2018), Currency. Rapid scaling.
21. **High Output Management** — Andy Grove (1983), Vintage. Management operations.
22. **The Great CEO Within** — Matt Mochary (2019). Operational excellence for leaders.
23. **Measure What Matters** — John Doerr (2018), Portfolio. OKRs.
24. **Radical Focus** — Christina Wodtke (2nd ed, 2021). OKR implementation.

### Change Management
25. **Switch** — Chip Heath & Dan Heath (2010), Crown. Change methodology.
26. **Leading Change** — John Kotter (2012), HBR Press. Change leadership.
27. **The Lean Change Method** — Jeff Anderson (2013). Agile change management.
28. **Accelerate** — John Kotter (2014), HBR Press. Dual operating system.

### Vendor and Procurement
29. **Strategic Sourcing Management** — Sherry Gordon (2008), Kogan Page.
30. **Procurement and Supply Chain Management** — Kenneth Lysons (10th ed, 2020), Pearson.

### Business Continuity
31. **Business Continuity Management** — Michael Blyth (2009), Wiley.
32. **The Resilient Enterprise** — Yossi Sheffi (2005), MIT Press. Supply chain resilience.
