# DevOps & Infrastructure Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (34)

### DevOps & SRE
1. **Site Reliability Engineering** — Google (2016), O'Reilly. The foundational SRE book.
2. **The Site Reliability Workbook** — Google (2018), O'Reilly. Practical SRE implementation.
3. **Building Secure and Reliable Systems** — Google (2020), O'Reilly. Security + reliability.
4. **Fundamentals of DevOps and Software Delivery** — Yevgeniy Brikman (2024), O'Reilly. Modern DevOps from Gruntwork.
5. **The Phoenix Project** — Gene Kim et al. (2013). DevOps novel, foundational story.
6. **The DevOps Handbook** — Gene Kim et al. (2nd ed, 2021). Practical DevOps implementation.
7. **Accelerate** — Nicole Forsgren et al. (2018). DORA metrics and high-performing teams.
8. **Team Topologies** — Matthew Skelton & Manuel Pais (2019). Organization design for fast flow.
9. **Mastering DevOps and Site Reliability Engineering** — (2024), Packt. Modern DevOps + SRE practices.

### Kubernetes & Containers
10. **The Kubernetes Book** — Nigel Poulton (2024 ed). Comprehensive K8s guide.
11. **Kubernetes Up and Running** — Burns, Beda, Hightower, Evenson (3rd ed, 2022), O'Reilly.
12. **Production Kubernetes** — Josh Rosso et al. (2021), O'Reilly. Enterprise K8s operations.
13. **Cloud Native DevOps with Kubernetes** — John Arundel & Justin Domingus (2nd ed, 2022), O'Reilly.
14. **The Book of Kubernetes** — Alan Hohn (2022), No Starch Press. K8s internals explained.
15. **Kubernetes Patterns** — Bilgin Ibryam & Roland Huß (2nd ed, 2023), O'Reilly. Design patterns for K8s.
16. **Istio in Action** — Christian Posta & Rinor Maloku (2022), Manning. Service mesh deep dive.

### Infrastructure as Code
17. **Terraform: Up & Running** — Yevgeniy Brikman (3rd ed, 2022), O'Reilly. Terraform best practices.
18. **Infrastructure as Code** — Kief Morris (2nd ed, 2020), O'Reilly. IaC principles and patterns.
19. **Pulumi in Action** — (2024). Modern IaC with real programming languages.

### Cloud Architecture
20. **AWS Well-Architected Framework** — AWS (continuously updated). Cloud architecture principles.
21. **Cloud Strategy** — Gregor Hohpe (2021). Enterprise cloud adoption.
22. **Designing Distributed Systems** — Brendan Burns (2018), O'Reilly. Container design patterns.
23. **Cloud Native Patterns** — Cornelia Davis (2019), Manning. Designing change-tolerant software.
24. **Architecting the Cloud** — Michael Kavis (2014), Wiley. Cloud design decisions.

### Observability & Monitoring
25. **Observability Engineering** — Charity Majors et al. (2022), O'Reilly. Modern observability practices.
26. **Distributed Tracing in Practice** — Austin Parker et al. (2020), O'Reilly. Tracing implementation.
27. **Practical Monitoring** — Mike Julian (2017), O'Reilly. Monitoring fundamentals.
28. **Systems Performance** — Brendan Gregg (2nd ed, 2020), Pearson. Performance analysis.

### Networking & Security
29. **Networking and Kubernetes** — James Strong & Vallery Lancey (2021), O'Reilly. K8s networking deep dive.
30. **Zero Trust Networks** — Evan Gilman & Doug Barth (2nd ed, 2024), O'Reilly. Zero trust architecture.
31. **Network Programmability and Automation** — Edelman, Lowe, Oswalt (2nd ed, 2023), O'Reilly.

### Database Operations
32. **PostgreSQL 16 Administration Cookbook** — (2024), Packt. Practical PostgreSQL DBA.
33. **High Performance MySQL** — Silvia Botros & Jeremy Tinley (4th ed, 2021), O'Reilly.
34. **Database Reliability Engineering** — Laine Campbell & Charity Majors (2017), O'Reilly.
