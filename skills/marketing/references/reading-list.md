# Marketing Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (34)

### Marketing Strategy
1. **This Is Marketing** — Seth Godin (2018), Portfolio. Modern marketing philosophy.
2. **Positioning** — Al Ries & Jack Trout (2001), McGraw-Hill. Positioning fundamentals.
3. **Obviously Awesome** — April Dunford (2019). Product positioning mastery.
4. **Play Bigger** — Al Ramadan et al. (2016), Harper Business. Category design.
5. **The 22 Immutable Laws of Marketing** — Al Ries & Jack Trout (1993), Harper Business.
6. **Marketing Made Simple** — Donald Miller (2020), HarperCollins. StoryBrand framework.
7. **Alchemy** — Rory Sutherland (2019), William Morrow. Behavioral science in marketing.

### Digital Marketing
8. **Digital Marketing Strategy** — Simon Kingsnorth (3rd ed, 2022), Kogan Page. Comprehensive digital.
9. **Hacking Growth** — Sean Ellis & Morgan Brown (2017), Crown. Growth marketing methodology.
10. **Product-Led Growth** — Wes Bush (2019). PLG strategy.
11. **Traction** — Gabriel Weinberg & Justin Mares (2015), Portfolio. Channel testing framework.
12. **Dotcom Secrets** — Russell Brunson (2nd ed, 2020), Hay House. Funnel strategy.
13. **Oversubscribed** — Daniel Priestley (2nd ed, 2020), Capstone. Demand generation.

### Content Marketing
14. **They Ask, You Answer** — Marcus Sheridan (2nd ed, 2019), Wiley. Content strategy.
15. **Content Inc.** — Joe Pulizzi (2nd ed, 2021), McGraw-Hill. Content-first business.
16. **Everybody Writes** — Ann Handley (2nd ed, 2022), Wiley. Writing for marketing.
17. **Building a StoryBrand** — Donald Miller (2017), HarperCollins. Narrative framework.
18. **Content Strategy for the Web** — Kristina Halvorson (2nd ed, 2012), New Riders.

### SEO
19. **The Art of SEO** — Enge, Spencer, Stricchiola (4th ed, 2022), O'Reilly. Comprehensive SEO.
20. **Product-Led SEO** — Eli Schwartz (2022). SEO strategy for product companies.
21. **SEO 2025** — Adam Clarke (2024). Updated SEO tactics.

### Brand
22. **Building a Brand Story** — Donald Miller (2017), HarperCollins. Brand narrative.
23. **Branding: In Five and a Half Steps** — Michael Johnson (2016), Thames & Hudson.
24. **The Brand Gap** — Marty Neumeier (2005), New Riders. Brand strategy essentials.
25. **Zag** — Marty Neumeier (2007), New Riders. Radical differentiation.

### Analytics & Data
26. **Lean Analytics** — Alistair Croll & Benjamin Yoskovitz (2013), O'Reilly. Metrics for startups.
27. **Marketing Analytics** — Mike Grigsby (2nd ed, 2022), Kogan Page. Data-driven marketing.
28. **Predictive Marketing** — Omer Artun & Dominique Levin (2015), Wiley. Predictive analytics.

### Email & Automation
29. **Email Marketing Rules** — Chad White (4th ed, 2022). Email best practices.
30. **Marketing Automation For Dummies** — Mathew Sweezey (2014), Wiley.

### Social Media
31. **Jab, Jab, Jab, Right Hook** — Gary Vaynerchuk (2013), Harper Business. Social content.
32. **Contagious** — Jonah Berger (2013), Simon & Schuster. Why things go viral.

### B2B Marketing
33. **The SaaS Playbook** — Rob Walling (2023). SaaS marketing and growth.
34. **From Impossible to Inevitable** — Aaron Ross & Jason Lemkin (2nd ed, 2019), Wiley. B2B growth.
