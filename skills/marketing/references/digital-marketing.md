## 1. Channel Strategy

### Channel Selection Framework

| Channel | Best For | Cost | Time to Results |
|---|---|---|---|
| SEO/Content | Long-term organic growth | Medium | 6-12 months |
| Paid Search (SEM) | High-intent demand capture | High | Immediate |
| Paid Social | Awareness, retargeting | Medium-High | 1-4 weeks |
| Email | Nurture, retention, upsell | Low | 1-4 weeks |
| Social organic | Brand, community, engagement | Low (time) | 3-6 months |
| Partnerships | Co-marketing, distribution | Variable | 2-6 months |
| Events | Enterprise, relationships | High | 1-3 months |
| Product-led | Viral, word-of-mouth | Low | Variable |
| Influencer | Awareness, credibility | Medium-High | 1-4 weeks |

### B2B vs B2C Channel Mix

| Channel | B2B Priority | B2C Priority |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | High | Low |
| Google Ads | High | High |
| Content/SEO | High | High |
| Email | High | Medium |
| Instagram/TikTok | Low | High |
| Events/Webinars | High | Low |
| Influencer | Medium | High |
| Referral | High | High |

## 2. Content Marketing

### Content Strategy Framework

| Component | Question | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Audience | Who are we creating for? | Personas, segments |
| Goals | What business outcome? | KPIs, targets |
| Topics | What do they care about? | Topic clusters, keywords |
| Formats | How do they consume? | Blog, video, podcast, etc. |
| Distribution | Where do they discover? | Channels, promotion plan |
| Measurement | How do we know it works? | Metrics, attribution |

### Content Types by Funnel Stage

| Stage | Goal | Content Types |
|---|---|---|
| Awareness (TOFU) | Attract, educate | Blog posts, videos, infographics, social |
| Consideration (MOFU) | Engage, nurture | Whitepapers, webinars, case studies, guides |
| Decision (BOFU) | Convert | Demos, free trials, comparison pages, ROI calculators |
| Retention | Retain, expand | Tutorials, community, newsletters, product updates |

### Content Calendar

| Element | Description | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Pillar content | Comprehensive, cornerstone pieces | Monthly |
| Blog posts | Regular, keyword-targeted | 2-4x/week |
| Social posts | Channel-specific, engaging | Daily |
| Email newsletters | Curated value delivery | Weekly/biweekly |
| Video content | Educational, entertaining | Weekly |
| Gated content | Lead magnets (guides, reports) | Monthly |

## 3. Paid Media

### Google Ads Structure

```
Account → Campaign → Ad Group → Ads + Keywords

Campaign types:
- Search: Text ads on search results (high intent)
- Display: Banner ads across websites (awareness)
- Shopping: Product listings (e-commerce)
- Video: YouTube ads (awareness, consideration)
- Performance Max: AI-optimized across all channels
```

### Paid Media Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark (varies) |
|---|---|---|
| CTR | Clicks / Impressions | 2-5% (search), 0.5-1% (display) |
| CPC | Cost / Clicks | $1-5 (B2B), $0.50-2 (B2C) |
| CPA | Cost / Conversions | Industry-specific |
| ROAS | Revenue / Ad Spend | 3-5x minimum |
| Quality Score | Relevance + CTR + Landing page | 7+ target |
| Impression Share | Your impressions / Available | 70%+ for brand |

### Retargeting Strategy

| Audience | Timing | Message |
|---|---|---|
| Website visitors (no action) | 1-7 days | Value proposition, social proof |
| Product page viewers | 1-14 days | Specific product, urgency |
| Cart abandoners | 1-3 days | Reminder, incentive |
| Free trial users | During trial | Feature highlights, upgrade |
| Past customers | 30-90 days | New features, upsell |

## 4. Email Marketing

### Email Types

| Type | Purpose | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Welcome series | Onboard, activate | Triggered (3-7 emails) |
| Newsletter | Nurture, engage | Weekly/biweekly |
| Product updates | Inform, re-engage | Per release |
| Promotional | Drive action | 2-4x/month max |
| Transactional | Confirm actions | Triggered |
| Re-engagement | Win back inactive | After 30-90 days inactivity |
| Lifecycle | Stage-appropriate nurture | Triggered by behavior |

### Email Best Practices

| Element | Best Practice | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Subject line | 6-10 words, curiosity or value | Open rate |
| Preview text | Complement subject, add context | Open rate |
| Personalization | Name, company, behavior-based | Engagement |
| CTA | Single, clear, action-oriented | Click rate |
| Mobile | Responsive, single column | 60%+ open on mobile |
| Timing | Test by audience (Tue-Thu often best) | Open rate |
| Segmentation | Behavior, stage, interest-based | All metrics |

### Email Metrics

| Metric | Good | Excellent |
|---|---|---|
| Open rate | 20-25% | 30%+ |
| Click rate | 2-3% | 5%+ |
| Click-to-open rate | 10-15% | 20%+ |
| Unsubscribe rate | <0.5% | <0.2% |
| Bounce rate | <2% | <0.5% |
| List growth rate | 2-5%/month | 5%+/month |

## 5. Social Media

### Platform Strategy

| Platform | Audience | Content Type | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | B2B professionals | Thought leadership, industry | B2B, recruiting, authority |
| Twitter/X | Tech, media, opinion | Short-form, news, threads | Real-time, community |
| Instagram | Visual, lifestyle | Photos, Reels, Stories | B2C, brand, visual products |
| TikTok | Gen Z, discovery | Short video, trends | Awareness, viral, young audience |
| YouTube | All demographics | Long-form video, tutorials | Education, SEO, evergreen |
| Reddit | Niche communities | Discussion, value-first | Community, research, authentic |

### Social Media Metrics

| Metric | Description | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| Engagement rate | (Likes + comments + shares) / Followers | Content resonance |
| Reach | Unique users who saw content | Awareness |
| Share of voice | Your mentions / Total category mentions | Market presence |
| Follower growth | Net new followers per period | Audience building |
| Click-through rate | Clicks / Impressions | Traffic driving |
| Conversion rate | Conversions from social | Business impact |

## 6. Demand Generation

### Demand Gen Funnel

```
Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Intent → Evaluation → Purchase
     ↓          ↓           ↓            ↓          ↓           ↓
  Content    Webinars    Case studies   Demo     Proposal    Close
  Ads        Guides      Comparisons   Trial    Negotiation  Onboard
```

### Lead Scoring Model

| Category | Signal | Points |
|---|---|---|
| Demographic | Job title match | +20 |
| Demographic | Company size fit | +15 |
| Demographic | Industry match | +10 |
| Behavioral | Visited pricing page | +25 |
| Behavioral | Downloaded whitepaper | +15 |
| Behavioral | Attended webinar | +20 |
| Behavioral | Requested demo | +40 |
| Negative | Competitor domain | -50 |
| Negative | Student email | -30 |
| Negative | No engagement 30 days | -10 |

### MQL to SQL Handoff

| Stage | Definition | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Lead | Known contact, minimal engagement | Marketing |
| MQL | Meets score threshold, shows intent | Marketing |
| SAL | Sales accepted, agrees to work | Sales |
| SQL | Qualified by sales, real opportunity | Sales |
| Opportunity | In pipeline, active deal | Sales |
