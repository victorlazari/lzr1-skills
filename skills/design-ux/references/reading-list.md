# Design & UX Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (33)

### UX Design Fundamentals
1. **The Design of Everyday Things** — Don Norman (revised ed, 2013), Basic Books. Foundational UX principles.
2. **Don't Make Me Think** — Steve Krug (3rd ed, 2014), New Riders. Web usability essentials.
3. **About Face: The Essentials of Interaction Design** — Alan Cooper et al. (4th ed, 2014), Wiley.
4. **The Elements of User Experience** — Jesse James Garrett (2nd ed, 2010), New Riders.
5. **Lean UX** — Jeff Gothelf & Josh Seiden (3rd ed, 2021), O'Reilly. Agile UX process.
6. **Sprint** — Jake Knapp (2016), Simon & Schuster. Design sprint methodology.
7. **Articulating Design Decisions** — Tom Greever (2nd ed, 2020), O'Reilly. Communicating design.

### User Research
8. **Just Enough Research** — Erika Hall (2nd ed, 2019), A Book Apart. Practical research guide.
9. **Interviewing Users** — Steve Portigal (2nd ed, 2023), Rosenfeld Media. User interview mastery.
10. **Observing the User Experience** — Goodman, Kuniavsky, Moed (2nd ed, 2012), Morgan Kaufmann.
11. **Quantifying the User Experience** — Jeff Sauro & James Lewis (2nd ed, 2016), Morgan Kaufmann.
12. **Continuous Discovery Habits** — Teresa Torres (2021), Product Talk. Ongoing research practice.
13. **Surveys That Work** — Caroline Jarrett (2021), Rosenfeld Media. Survey design.

### Visual & UI Design
14. **Refactoring UI** — Adam Wathan & Steve Schoger (2018). Practical UI design for developers.
15. **The Non-Designer's Design Book** — Robin Williams (5th ed, 2024), Peachpit. Design basics.
16. **Grid Systems in Graphic Design** — Josef Müller-Brockmann (1996), Niggli. Grid fundamentals.
17. **Thinking with Type** — Ellen Lupton (3rd ed, 2024), Princeton Architectural Press. Typography.
18. **Color and Light** — James Gurney (2010), Andrews McMeel. Color theory.
19. **Layout Essentials** — Beth Tondreau (2019), Rockport. Layout design patterns.

### Design Systems
20. **Design Systems** — Alla Kholmatova (2017), Smashing Magazine. Building design systems.
21. **Atomic Design** — Brad Frost (2016). Component-based design methodology.
22. **Expressive Design Systems** — Yesenia Perez-Cruz (2019), A Book Apart. Brand in systems.
23. **Design System Handbook** — InVision (2017). Practical design system guide.

### Accessibility
24. **Inclusive Design Patterns** — Heydon Pickering (2016), Smashing Magazine. Accessible components.
25. **A Web for Everyone** — Sarah Horton & Whitney Quesenbery (2014), Rosenfeld Media.
26. **Accessibility for Everyone** — Laura Kalbag (2017), A Book Apart. Accessibility primer.
27. **Inclusive Components** — Heydon Pickering (2019). Accessible UI patterns.

### Information Architecture
28. **Information Architecture** — Rosenfeld, Morville, Arango (4th ed, 2015), O'Reilly. IA bible.
29. **How to Make Sense of Any Mess** — Abby Covert (2014). IA for everyone.
30. **Everyday Information Architecture** — Lisa Maria Marquis (2019), A Book Apart.

### Interaction & Motion
31. **Microinteractions** — Dan Saffer (2013), O'Reilly. Designing details.
32. **Designing Interface Animation** — Val Head (2016), Rosenfeld Media. Motion design.
33. **Animation at Work** — Rachel Nabors (2017), A Book Apart. Web animation.
