# Data & Analytics Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (34)

### Data Engineering
1. **Fundamentals of Data Engineering** — Joe Reis & Matt Housley (2022), O'Reilly. Modern data engineering foundations.
2. **Designing Data-Intensive Applications** — Martin Kleppmann (2017), O'Reilly. Distributed systems for data.
3. **Data Pipelines Pocket Reference** — James Densmore (2021), O'Reilly. Pipeline patterns.
4. **Streaming Systems** — Tyler Akidau et al. (2018), O'Reilly. Stream processing theory.
5. **The Data Warehouse Toolkit** — Ralph Kimball & Margy Ross (3rd ed, 2013), Wiley. Dimensional modeling bible.
6. **Data Engineering with dbt** — Roberto Zagni (2023), Packt. dbt-centric data engineering.
7. **Apache Spark: The Definitive Guide** — Chambers & Zaharia (2018), O'Reilly. Spark deep dive.
8. **Data Mesh** — Zhamak Dehghani (2022), O'Reilly. Decentralized data architecture.

### Data Analysis & Science
9. **Python for Data Analysis** — Wes McKinney (3rd ed, 2022), O'Reilly. pandas creator's guide.
10. **Practical Statistics for Data Scientists** — Bruce, Bruce & Gedeck (2nd ed, 2020), O'Reilly.
11. **Storytelling with Data** — Cole Nussbaumer Knaflic (2015), Wiley. Data visualization communication.
12. **Storytelling with Data: Let's Practice** — Cole Nussbaumer Knaflic (2019), Wiley. Hands-on exercises.
13. **Naked Statistics** — Charles Wheelan (2013), Norton. Statistics for non-statisticians.
14. **Trustworthy Online Controlled Experiments** — Kohavi, Tang & Xu (2020), Cambridge. A/B testing bible.
15. **The Art of Statistics** — David Spiegelhalter (2019), Basic Books. Modern statistical thinking.
16. **Thinking with Data** — Max Shron (2014), O'Reilly. Analytical problem framing.

### SQL & Databases
17. **Learning SQL** — Alan Beaulieu (3rd ed, 2020), O'Reilly. SQL fundamentals.
18. **SQL for Data Scientists** — Renee Teate (2021), Wiley. Analytical SQL patterns.
19. **High Performance MySQL** — Silvia Botros & Jeremy Tinley (4th ed, 2021), O'Reilly.
20. **The Art of PostgreSQL** — Dimitri Fontaine (2nd ed, 2022). Advanced PostgreSQL.

### Business Intelligence & Visualization
21. **The Big Book of Dashboards** — Wexler, Shaffer & Cotgreave (2017), Wiley. Dashboard patterns.
22. **Information Dashboard Design** — Stephen Few (2nd ed, 2013). Dashboard principles.
23. **Show Me the Numbers** — Stephen Few (2012). Table and graph design.
24. **Fundamentals of Data Visualization** — Claus Wilke (2019), O'Reilly. Visualization theory.
25. **Better Data Visualizations** — Jonathan Schwabish (2021), Columbia. Modern viz guide.

### Data Governance & Quality
26. **Data Quality Fundamentals** — Barr Moses et al. (2022), O'Reilly. Data observability.
27. **Data Governance: The Definitive Guide** — Evren Eryurek et al. (2021), O'Reilly.
28. **The Data Catalog** — Mark Peco & Andrew Beaulieu (2023). Modern data cataloging.
29. **Data Management at Scale** — Piethein Strengholt (2nd ed, 2023), O'Reilly. Enterprise data architecture.

### Analytics Engineering
30. **Analytics Engineering with SQL and dbt** — Rui Machado & Hélder Russa (2022), O'Reilly.
31. **Data Modeling Made Simple** — Steve Hoberman (3rd ed, 2015). Data modeling fundamentals.
32. **Building a Scalable Data Warehouse with Data Vault 2.0** — Dan Linstedt & Michael Olschimke (2015).

### Modern Data Stack
33. **Trino: The Definitive Guide** — Matt Fuller et al. (2021), O'Reilly. Distributed SQL engine.
34. **Delta Lake: The Definitive Guide** — Denny Lee et al. (2024), O'Reilly. Lakehouse format.
