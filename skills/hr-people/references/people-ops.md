## 1. Onboarding

### Onboarding Timeline

| Timing | Activities | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-day 1 | Welcome email, equipment, accounts, buddy assignment | People Ops |
| Day 1 | Orientation, setup, team intro, first 1:1 with manager | People Ops + Manager |
| Week 1 | Company overview, product deep-dive, meet stakeholders | Manager + Buddy |
| Month 1 | Role-specific training, first deliverable, 30-day check-in | Manager |
| Month 2 | Increasing independence, cross-team relationships | Manager |
| Month 3 | Full productivity expected, 90-day review | Manager |

### Onboarding Checklist

| Category | Items |
|---|---|
| IT/Access | Laptop, email, Slack, GitHub, VPN, SSO apps |
| HR/Admin | Paperwork, benefits enrollment, payroll, handbook |
| Team | Buddy assigned, team intros, recurring meetings |
| Role | Goals set, projects assigned, expectations clear |
| Culture | Values session, ERG intros, social events |
| Learning | Product training, tool training, documentation |

## 2. Offboarding

### Offboarding Process

| Timing | Activity | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Resignation received | Accept, determine last day, notify HR | Manager |
| Same day | IT notified, access review scheduled | People Ops |
| 1-2 weeks before | Knowledge transfer plan | Manager |
| Last week | Exit interview, return equipment | People Ops |
| Last day | Access revoked, final pay processed | IT + Payroll |
| Post-departure | Team communication, role backfill | Manager + Recruiting |

### Exit Interview Questions

| Category | Questions |
|---|---|
| Reason | What prompted your decision to leave? |
| Role | Was the role what you expected? What would you change? |
| Manager | How was your relationship with your manager? |
| Growth | Did you feel you had growth opportunities? |
| Culture | How would you describe the culture? |
| Compensation | Was compensation a factor in your decision? |
| Recommendation | Would you recommend this company to others? |
| Improvement | What one thing would you change? |

## 3. HR Compliance

### Key Employment Laws (US)

| Law | Requirement | Applies To |
|---|---|---|
| FLSA | Minimum wage, overtime, classification | All employers |
| FMLA | 12 weeks unpaid leave | 50+ employees |
| ADA | Disability accommodation | 15+ employees |
| Title VII | Anti-discrimination | 15+ employees |
| ADEA | Age discrimination protection | 20+ employees |
| WARN Act | 60-day layoff notice | 100+ employees |
| COBRA | Health insurance continuation | 20+ employees |
| ERISA | Retirement plan requirements | Plans offered |
| State laws | Vary by state (often stricter) | State-specific |

### HR Policy Essentials

| Policy | Content | Update Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Employee handbook | All policies, expectations, benefits | Annual |
| Anti-harassment | Definition, reporting, consequences | Annual |
| Remote work | Expectations, equipment, expenses | As needed |
| PTO/Leave | Accrual, usage, approval | Annual |
| Code of conduct | Ethics, behavior expectations | Annual |
| Data privacy | Employee data handling | Annual |
| Social media | Guidelines for public communication | As needed |

### HRIS Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Description | Weight |
|---|---|---|
| Core HR | Employee records, org chart, reporting | High |
| Payroll integration | Seamless payroll processing | High |
| Self-service | Employee and manager portals | High |
| Scalability | Grows with company (50 → 500+) | High |
| Integrations | Connects to other tools | Medium |
| Compliance | Built-in compliance features | Medium |
| Analytics | Reporting and dashboards | Medium |
| Cost | Per-employee pricing | Medium |

## 5. People Analytics

### Key People Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Turnover rate | Departures / Average headcount × 100 | <15% annually (tech) |
| Voluntary turnover | Voluntary departures / Average headcount | <10% |
| Regrettable turnover | High-performer departures / Total departures | <30% |
| Time to fill | Days from req open to offer accepted | 30-60 days |
| Offer acceptance rate | Offers accepted / Offers extended | >85% |
| Employee NPS | Promoters - Detractors | >30 |
| Engagement score | Survey composite score | >70% favorable |
| Manager effectiveness | Manager survey scores | >75% favorable |
| Diversity ratios | % by gender, ethnicity, level | Improving trend |
| Revenue per employee | Total revenue / Headcount | Growing |

### Workforce Planning

| Activity | Frequency | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Headcount forecasting | Quarterly | Hiring plan by department |
| Attrition modeling | Monthly | Predicted departures |
| Skills gap analysis | Semi-annual | Training needs, hiring priorities |
| Succession planning | Annual | Bench strength for key roles |
| Org design review | Annual | Structure optimization |
| Compensation benchmarking | Annual | Market competitiveness |
