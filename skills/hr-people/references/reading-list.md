# HR & People Operations Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (32)

### People Strategy & Leadership
1. **Work Rules!** — Laszlo Bock (2015), Twelve. Google's people practices.
2. **Powerful** — Patty McCord (2018), Missionday. Netflix culture principles.
3. **The Culture Code** — Daniel Coyle (2018), Bantam. Building great cultures.
4. **No Rules Rules** — Reed Hastings & Erin Meyer (2020), Penguin. Netflix culture.
5. **An Everyone Culture** — Robert Kegan & Lisa Lahey (2016), HBR Press. Deliberately developmental orgs.
6. **Radical Candor** — Kim Scott (2017), St. Martin's. Feedback culture.
7. **The Making of a Manager** — Julie Zhuo (2019), Portfolio. New manager guide.

### Talent Acquisition
8. **Who** — Geoff Smart & Randy Street (2008), Ballantine. Hiring methodology.
9. **Talent Wins** — Ram Charan et al. (2018), HBR Press. Talent strategy.
10. **Hiring for Attitude** — Mark Murphy (2012), McGraw-Hill. Cultural fit hiring.
11. **The Talent Delusion** — Tomas Chamorro-Premuzic (2017), Piatkus. Science of hiring.
12. **Recruit Rockstars** — Jeff Hyman (2017). A-player hiring.

### Organizational Design
13. **Team Topologies** — Matthew Skelton & Manuel Pais (2019), IT Revolution. Team design.
14. **An Elegant Puzzle** — Will Larson (2019), Stripe Press. Engineering management.
15. **The Staff Engineer's Path** — Tanya Reilly (2022), O'Reilly. IC career growth.
16. **Org Design for Design Orgs** — Peter Merholz & Kristin Skinner (2016), O'Reilly.
17. **Reorg** — Stephen Heidari-Robinson & Suzanne Heywood (2016), HBR Press.

### Performance & Development
18. **Nine Lies About Work** — Marcus Buckingham & Ashley Goodall (2019), HBR Press.
19. **Measure What Matters** — John Doerr (2018), Portfolio. OKRs.
20. **Thanks for the Feedback** — Douglas Stone & Sheila Heen (2014), Penguin.
21. **The Coaching Habit** — Michael Bungay Stanier (2016), Box of Crayons.
22. **Drive** — Daniel Pink (2009), Riverhead. Motivation science.

### Compensation & Benefits
23. **Compensation** — George Milkovich et al. (13th ed, 2023), McGraw-Hill. Comp textbook.
24. **Strategic Compensation** — Joseph Martocchio (10th ed, 2022), Pearson.
25. **The Equity Handbook** — Carta (2024, online). Startup equity guide.

### DEI & Belonging
26. **Inclusion on Purpose** — Ruchika Tulshyan (2022), MIT Press. Inclusive leadership.
27. **Belonging** — Sue Unerman et al. (2020), Bloomsbury. Workplace belonging.
28. **Better Allies** — Karen Catlin (2nd ed, 2021). Actionable allyship.

### HR Operations
29. **The HR Scorecard** — Becker, Huselid & Ulrich (2001), HBR Press. HR metrics.
30. **People Analytics** — Jonathan Ferrar & David Green (2021), Kogan Page.
31. **Predictive HR Analytics** — Martin Edwards & Kirsten Edwards (2nd ed, 2019), Kogan Page.
32. **HR from the Outside In** — Dave Ulrich et al. (2012), McGraw-Hill.
