# Content & Communications Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (32)

### Content Strategy
1. **Content Strategy for the Web** — Kristina Halvorson (2nd ed, 2012), New Riders.
2. **Everybody Writes** — Ann Handley (2nd ed, 2022), Wiley. Writing for business.
3. **They Ask, You Answer** — Marcus Sheridan (2nd ed, 2019), Wiley. Content marketing.
4. **Content Inc.** — Joe Pulizzi (2nd ed, 2021), McGraw-Hill. Content-first business.
5. **Epic Content Marketing** — Joe Pulizzi (2nd ed, 2023), McGraw-Hill.
6. **Killing Marketing** — Joe Pulizzi & Robert Rose (2017), McGraw-Hill.

### Technical Writing
7. **Docs Like Code** — Anne Gentle (2017), Lulu. Documentation as code.
8. **The Product is Docs** — Christopher Gales (2020). Documentation strategy.
9. **Every Page is Page One** — Mark Baker (2013). Topic-based authoring.
10. **Living Documentation** — Cyrille Martraire (2019), Addison-Wesley.
11. **Developing Quality Technical Information** — IBM (3rd ed, 2014), IBM Press.
12. **DITA Best Practices** — Laura Bellamy et al. (2011), IBM Press.

### Copywriting
13. **Ogilvy on Advertising** — David Ogilvy (1985), Vintage. Advertising classic.
14. **The Copywriter's Handbook** — Robert Bly (4th ed, 2020), St. Martin's Griffin.
15. **Building a StoryBrand** — Donald Miller (2017), HarperCollins. Messaging framework.
16. **Made to Stick** — Chip Heath & Dan Heath (2007), Random House.
17. **Obviously Awesome** — April Dunford (2019). Positioning.
18. **The Adweek Copywriting Handbook** — Joseph Sugarman (2007), Wiley.

### Communications
19. **Strategic Communications Planning** — Laurie Wilson (7th ed, 2016), Routledge.
20. **The New Rules of Marketing and PR** — David Meerman Scott (8th ed, 2022), Wiley.
21. **Trust Me, I'm Lying** — Ryan Holiday (2012), Portfolio. Media manipulation.
22. **Spin Sucks** — Gini Dietrich (2014), Que. Ethical PR.

### Developer Relations
23. **Developer Relations** — Caroline Lewko & James Parton (2021). DevRel guide.
24. **The Business Value of Developer Relations** — Mary Thengvall (2018), Apress.
25. **Developer Marketing Does Not Exist** — Adam DuVander (2020). Dev marketing.
26. **Working in Public** — Nadia Eghbal (2020), Stripe Press. Open source communities.

### Writing Craft
27. **On Writing Well** — William Zinsser (30th ed, 2006), Harper Perennial.
28. **The Elements of Style** — Strunk & White (4th ed, 1999), Pearson.
29. **Bird by Bird** — Anne Lamott (1995), Anchor. Writing process.
30. **Several Short Sentences About Writing** — Verlyn Klinkenborg (2012), Vintage.
31. **Writing That Works** — Kenneth Roman & Joel Raphaelson (3rd ed, 2000), Harper.
32. **Microcopy: The Complete Guide** — Kinneret Yifrah (2017). UX writing.
