## 1. Content Strategy Framework

### Strategy Components

| Component | Description | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Audience | Who are we creating for? | Personas, segments |
| Goals | What business outcomes? | KPIs, targets |
| Audit | What content exists? | Content inventory |
| Gaps | What's missing? | Gap analysis |
| Pillars | Core themes and topics | Content pillars (3-5) |
| Channels | Where to publish? | Channel strategy |
| Governance | How to maintain quality? | Style guide, workflows |
| Measurement | How to track success? | Metrics framework |

### Content Pillars

```
Example for a B2B SaaS company:

Pillar 1: Product Education
  → How-to guides, tutorials, feature updates

Pillar 2: Industry Thought Leadership
  → Trends, research, expert opinions

Pillar 3: Customer Stories
  → Case studies, testimonials, interviews

Pillar 4: Technical Deep Dives
  → Architecture, engineering blog, benchmarks

Pillar 5: Company Culture
  → Team stories, values, behind-the-scenes
```

## 2. Editorial Planning

### Editorial Calendar Template

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Publish date | When it goes live |
| Title | Working title |
| Content type | Blog, video, podcast, etc. |
| Pillar | Which content pillar |
| Author | Who's writing |
| Status | Draft, review, approved, published |
| Target keyword | SEO target |
| CTA | Call to action |
| Distribution | Channels for promotion |

### Content Production Workflow

| Stage | Activities | Owner | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ideation | Topic research, keyword analysis | Strategist | Ongoing |
| Brief | Outline, requirements, references | Strategist | 1 day |
| Draft | First draft writing | Writer | 3-5 days |
| Review | Editorial review, fact-check | Editor | 1-2 days |
| Revision | Incorporate feedback | Writer | 1-2 days |
| Design | Graphics, formatting | Designer | 1-2 days |
| Approval | Final sign-off | Stakeholder | 1 day |
| Publish | Upload, SEO, formatting | Publisher | 1 day |
| Promote | Distribution, social, email | Marketing | Ongoing |

## 3. Content Types and Formats

### Content Format Selection

| Format | Best For | Effort | Shelf Life |
|---|---|---|---|
| Blog post | SEO, thought leadership | Medium | 6-12 months |
| Long-form guide | Authority, lead gen | High | 12-24 months |
| Case study | Social proof, sales enablement | Medium | 12-18 months |
| Video | Engagement, tutorials | High | 6-12 months |
| Podcast | Thought leadership, community | Medium | Evergreen |
| Infographic | Data visualization, sharing | Medium | 6-12 months |
| Webinar | Lead gen, education | High | 3-6 months |
| Newsletter | Retention, nurture | Low-Medium | 1 week |
| Social post | Awareness, engagement | Low | 1-3 days |
| White paper | Lead gen, authority | High | 12-24 months |

### Content Repurposing

```
One long-form piece → Multiple formats:

Blog post (2,000 words)
  → Twitter/LinkedIn thread (10 posts)
  → Newsletter excerpt
  → Infographic (key stats)
  → Short video (2 min summary)
  → Podcast episode (discussion)
  → Slide deck (presentation)
  → Email sequence (3-part series)
```

## 4. Distribution and Promotion

### Distribution Channels

| Channel | Type | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Company blog | Owned | SEO, authority |
| Email newsletter | Owned | Retention, nurture |
| LinkedIn | Earned/Paid | B2B, thought leadership |
| Twitter/X | Earned | Tech community, news |
| YouTube | Owned | Tutorials, demos |
| Dev.to / Hashnode | Earned | Developer audience |
| Hacker News | Earned | Tech audience |
| Product Hunt | Earned | Launches |
| Reddit | Earned | Community engagement |
| Paid social | Paid | Targeted reach |

### Promotion Checklist

```
Pre-publish:
□ SEO optimized (title, meta, headers)
□ Internal links added
□ CTA included
□ Social images created
□ Email segment identified

Publish day:
□ Published and indexed
□ Shared on company social
□ Shared by team members
□ Email to subscribers
□ Posted in relevant communities

Post-publish:
□ Monitor engagement
□ Respond to comments
□ Repurpose into other formats
□ Update based on feedback
□ Track performance metrics
```

## 5. Content Metrics

### Metrics by Goal

| Goal | Metrics | Tools |
|---|---|---|
| Awareness | Traffic, impressions, reach | Google Analytics, social analytics |
| Engagement | Time on page, shares, comments | GA, social platforms |
| Lead generation | Conversions, form fills, downloads | HubSpot, GA |
| SEO | Rankings, organic traffic, backlinks | Ahrefs, SEMrush |
| Revenue | Pipeline influenced, deals closed | CRM attribution |
| Retention | Return visitors, email opens | GA, email platform |

### Content Scoring

| Factor | Weight | Measurement |
|---|---|---|
| Traffic | 25% | Pageviews, unique visitors |
| Engagement | 25% | Time on page, scroll depth |
| Conversion | 30% | CTR, form submissions |
| SEO value | 20% | Rankings, backlinks earned |
