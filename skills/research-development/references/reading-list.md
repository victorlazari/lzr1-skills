# Research & Development Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (32)

### Innovation & R&D Management
1. **The Innovator's Dilemma** — Clayton Christensen (1997), HBR Press. Disruption theory.
2. **The Innovator's Solution** — Clayton Christensen (2003), HBR Press. Growth through innovation.
3. **The Lean Startup** — Eric Ries (2011), Crown. Validated learning.
4. **Loonshots** — Safi Bahcall (2019), St. Martin's Press. Nurturing breakthroughs.
5. **The Wide Lens** — Ron Adner (2012), Portfolio. Innovation ecosystems.
6. **Ten Types of Innovation** — Larry Keeley et al. (2013), Wiley.
7. **The Art of Innovation** — Tom Kelley (2001), Currency. IDEO's approach.
8. **Creative Confidence** — Tom Kelley & David Kelley (2013), Crown.

### Technology Strategy
9. **Technology Strategy for Managers and Entrepreneurs** — Scott Shane (2008), Pearson.
10. **The Technology Fallacy** — Kane et al. (2019), MIT Press. Digital transformation.
11. **Competing in the Age of AI** — Iansiti & Lakhani (2020), HBR Press.
12. **Platform Revolution** — Parker, Van Alstyne & Choudary (2016), Norton.
13. **The Second Machine Age** — Brynjolfsson & McAfee (2014), Norton.
14. **AI Superpowers** — Kai-Fu Lee (2018), Houghton Mifflin.

### Research Methodology
15. **Research Design** — John Creswell (6th ed, 2023), SAGE.
16. **The Craft of Research** — Booth, Colomb & Williams (4th ed, 2016), Chicago.
17. **How to Read a Paper** — Trisha Greenhalgh (6th ed, 2019), Wiley.
18. **Writing Science** — Joshua Schimel (2012), Oxford.
19. **The Structure of Scientific Revolutions** — Thomas Kuhn (1962), Chicago.

### Emerging Technologies
20. **Life 3.0** — Max Tegmark (2017), Knopf. AI and the future.
21. **The Age of AI** — Kissinger, Schmidt & Huttenlocher (2021), Little Brown.
22. **Quantum Computing: An Applied Approach** — Hidary (2nd ed, 2021), Springer.
23. **The Metaverse** — Matthew Ball (2022), Liveright.
24. **Chip War** — Chris Miller (2022), Scribner. Semiconductor industry.

### Systems Thinking
25. **Thinking in Systems** — Donella Meadows (2008), Chelsea Green.
26. **The Fifth Discipline** — Peter Senge (1990), Currency. Learning organizations.
27. **Antifragile** — Nassim Taleb (2012), Random House.
28. **Complexity** — M. Mitchell Waldrop (1992), Simon & Schuster.

### Experimentation
29. **Trustworthy Online Controlled Experiments** — Kohavi, Tang & Xu (2020), Cambridge.
30. **The Lean Product Playbook** — Dan Olsen (2015), Wiley.
31. **Sprint** — Jake Knapp (2016), Simon & Schuster. Design sprints.
32. **Testing Business Ideas** — Bland & Osterwalder (2019), Wiley.
