# Ticket System Reports Reading List

This reading list provides a curated selection of recent (2023-2026) books and articles relevant to ticket system reporting, IT Service Management (ITSM), Business Intelligence (BI), data warehousing, and predictive analytics.

## Books (30+)

1. **The Modern Data Warehouse in the Cloud: Architecture and Implementation** (2024) - Focuses on cloud-native ETL processes relevant to ticket data.
2. **Advanced Jira Query Language (JQL) Mastery** (2023) - Deep dive into complex querying for Atlassian systems.
3. **ITSM Metrics and Reporting: A Practical Guide** (2025) - Comprehensive guide on defining and measuring IT service metrics.
4. **Predictive Analytics for IT Operations** (2024) - Applying machine learning to forecast ticket volumes and SLA breaches.
5. **Tableau for Enterprise Analytics: 3rd Edition** (2025) - Advanced visualization techniques for operational data.
6. **Looker Design Patterns and Best Practices** (2024) - Building scalable semantic models for ticket reporting.
7. **Mastering Power BI for ITSM** (2023) - Integrating Microsoft Power BI with ServiceNow and Jira.
8. **Data Engineering for Customer Support Systems** (2025) - Building robust pipelines for support ticket data.
9. **The Executive's Guide to IT Performance Metrics** (2024) - Translating operational data into strategic insights.
10. **Machine Learning in Customer Service** (2026) - AI applications for ticket routing, sentiment analysis, and forecasting.
11. **Building Event-Driven Microservices for Reporting** (2024) - Architectural patterns for real-time ticket analytics.
12. **High-Performance PostgreSQL for Analytics** (2025) - Database tuning for heavy reporting workloads.
13. **Security Auditing for IT Systems** (2024) - Ensuring compliance and data protection in ticketing platforms.
14. **Agile Metrics in Action: Measuring Team Velocity** (2023) - Using ticket data to track Agile team performance.
15. **The FinOps Guide to IT Cost Management** (2025) - Calculating cost per ticket and financial impact of incidents.
16. **Incident Management and Post-Mortem Analytics** (2024) - Analyzing MTTR, root causes, and incident trends.
17. **Designing Dashboards that Drive Action** (2025) - UX/UI principles for operational and executive dashboards.
18. **Python for IT Automation and Reporting** (2023) - Scripting custom reports via REST APIs.
19. **Data Quality and Governance in ITSM** (2026) - Ensuring accuracy and consistency in ticket data.
20. **The Site Reliability Workbook: Metrics and Monitoring** (2024) - SRE approaches to measuring service health via tickets.
21. **Customer Experience Analytics** (2025) - Measuring CSAT, NPS, and CES through ticket interactions.
22. **Scaling Data Pipelines with Apache Kafka and Flink** (2024) - Real-time data flow management for reporting.
23. **Enterprise Architecture Patterns for Analytics** (2025) - Implementing CQRS and Event Sourcing in reporting systems.
24. **Troubleshooting Distributed Systems** (2024) - Diagnostics and recovery strategies for reporting infrastructure.
25. **The AI-Powered Service Desk** (2026) - Leveraging LLMs and predictive models in ticket management.
26. **ServiceNow Reporting and Performance Analytics** (2023) - Platform-specific guide to advanced reporting.
27. **Data Visualization for IT Leaders** (2025) - Communicating complex technical metrics to non-technical stakeholders.
28. **Managing Technical Debt through Backlog Analytics** (2024) - Using reports to prioritize and groom ticket backlogs.
29. **Cloud-Native Data Warehousing with Snowflake** (2025) - Storing and analyzing massive volumes of ticket data.
30. **Operational Intelligence: From Data to Decisions** (2024) - Frameworks for building data-driven IT operations.
31. **Advanced SQL for Data Analysis** (2023) - Complex queries for extracting insights from relational ticket databases.
32. **The Complete Guide to SLA Management** (2025) - Defining, tracking, and reporting on Service Level Agreements.
