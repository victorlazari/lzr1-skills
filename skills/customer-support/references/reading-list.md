# Customer Support Recommended Reading (2024-2026)

## Essential Books (32)

### Customer Service Fundamentals
1. **The Effortless Experience** — Dixon, Toman & DeLisi (2013), Portfolio. Reducing customer effort.
2. **Delivering Happiness** — Tony Hsieh (2010), Grand Central. Zappos service culture.
3. **Hug Your Haters** — Jay Baer (2016), Portfolio. Handling complaints.
4. **The Thank You Economy** — Gary Vaynerchuk (2011), Harper Business.
5. **Unreasonable Hospitality** — Will Guidara (2022), Optimism Press. Service excellence.
6. **Be Our Guest** — Disney Institute (2011), Disney Editions. Disney service model.

### Customer Success
7. **Customer Success** — Nick Mehta et al. (2016), Wiley. CS fundamentals.
8. **The Customer Success Economy** — Nick Mehta & Allison Pickens (2020), Wiley.
9. **Farm Don't Hunt** — Guy Nirpaz (2016). Customer success for SaaS.
10. **Practical Customer Success Management** — Rick Adams (2019), Routledge.
11. **Customer Success Professional's Handbook** — Ashvin Vaidyanathan (2020), Wiley.

### Technical Support
12. **The Art of Support** — Francoise Tourniaire (2016). Support operations.
13. **Technical Support Essentials** — Andrew Sanchez (2010), Apress.
14. **Building a World-Class Support Organization** — various (2023). Modern support.
15. **IT Service Management** — ITIL Foundation (2019). ITSM framework.

### Knowledge Management
16. **Every Page is Page One** — Mark Baker (2013). Topic-based documentation.
17. **Docs Like Code** — Anne Gentle (2017), Lulu. Documentation as code.
18. **The Product is Docs** — Christopher Gales (2020). Documentation strategy.
19. **Living Documentation** — Cyrille Martraire (2019), Addison-Wesley.
20. **Don't Make Me Think** — Steve Krug (3rd ed, 2014), New Riders. Usability.

### Communication
21. **Crucial Conversations** — Patterson et al. (3rd ed, 2021), McGraw-Hill.
22. **Nonviolent Communication** — Marshall Rosenberg (2015), PuddleDancer.
23. **Difficult Conversations** — Stone, Patton & Heen (2010), Penguin.
24. **Never Split the Difference** — Chris Voss (2016), Harper Business. Negotiation.

### Operations and Scaling
25. **The Lean Startup** — Eric Ries (2011), Crown. Lean methodology.
26. **Scaling Up** — Verne Harnish (2014), Gazelles. Growth operations.
27. **High Output Management** — Andy Grove (1983), Vintage. Management.
28. **The Hard Thing About Hard Things** — Ben Horowitz (2014), Harper Business.

### AI and Automation in Support
29. **AI Customer Service** — various (2024). AI-powered support.
30. **Conversational AI** — various (2023). Chatbot and AI design.
31. **Human + Machine** — Paul Daugherty & H. James Wilson (2018), HBR Press.
32. **The AI-First Company** — Ash Fontana (2021), Portfolio. AI in business.
